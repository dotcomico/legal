סיכום בעברית — תקציר מדיניות הפרטיות

האפליקציה קשניק אינה אוספת, שולחת, או מוכרת שום מידע אישי לגורמים חיצוניים.

כל הנתונים שאתה מזין (קוד ארון, מספר תא, מספר ארון, מערכת שיעורים) נשמרים על המכשיר שלך בלבד תחת הצפנה. הם אינם נשלחים לשום שרת.

האפליקציה פותחת דף אינטרנט חיצוני אחד (אתר יזכור — ksharet.org.il) וכן קישורים לחנות Google Play, לאינסטגרם ולאתר talilocker.com. כאשר אתה לוחץ על קישורים אלו, מדיניות הפרטיות של אותם אתרים חלה.

האפליקציה מורשית לשלוח לך התראות (נוטיפיקציות) עם מסלול החדר שחיפשת. מידע זה אינו יוצא מהמכשיר שלך.

האפליקציה מיועדת לתלמידי תיכון. אנו אינם אוספים מידע מאף משתמש, לרבות קטינים.

4. Local Data Storage and Encryption

4.1 Encrypted Storage (Locker Data)

Your locker code, cabinet number, and cell number are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences API, provided by the androidx.security:security-crypto library. This implementation uses:

  • Preference keys encrypted with AES-256-SIV
  • Preference values encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Master encryption key stored in the Android Keystore, which is hardware-backed on supported devices

The Developer cannot decrypt this data. It exists only on your Device and is inaccessible to any third party without physical access to your unlocked Device.

4.2 Unencrypted Local Storage (Schedule and Preferences)

Your class schedule, room preferences, and display settings are stored using standard Android SharedPreferences. This data is accessible within the App's private storage sandbox on your Device and is not accessible to other apps without root access. It is not transmitted or backed up to any server operated by the Developer.

4.3 Migration

If you previously used an older version of the App that stored locker data in unencrypted form, the App automatically migrates that data into encrypted storage upon first launch of a newer version. The unencrypted copy is deleted after migration.

5. Notifications

When you look up a room's navigation route, the App may send a local push notification to your Device displaying the route details. This notification:

  • Is generated entirely on-Device — no data is sent to any server to produce it
  • Contains only the room number and route text you searched for
  • Is marked VISIBILITY_PRIVATE, meaning its content is hidden from your Device's lock screen
  • Triggers a vibration pattern ([0, 300, 200, 300] ms) via the VIBRATE permission

You can disable notifications for the App at any time through your Device's system settings (Settings → Apps → Kashnik → Notifications).

6. Clipboard Access

The App can write room route information to your Device's clipboard when you tap the "Copy" action. This is a one-way write operation initiated by you. The App does not read from the clipboard and does not access any other clipboard content.

8. Android Backup

The App's manifest has android:allowBackup="true" enabled. This means Android may back up the App's unencrypted SharedPreferences data (schedule, display preferences) to your Google account as part of Android's standard device backup feature.

Encrypted data (locker code, cabinet, cell number) is excluded from Android backup by design — the Android Keystore keys used for encryption are non-exportable and cannot be restored to a different device, making encrypted data effectively irretrievable from backup.

Control over Android backup is in your Device's system settings (Settings → Google → Backup). The Developer does not independently receive or access any backup data.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

11.1 Retention

All data stored by the App persists on your Device for as long as the App remains installed. There is no server-side retention because no data is transmitted to the Developer.

11.2 Deletion by You

You can delete all locally stored data in any of the following ways:

  • In-App: Navigate to Settings → "מחיקת כל הנתונים" (Delete All Data). This clears all SharedPreferences, including encrypted storage.
  • Device Settings: Go to Settings → Apps → Kashnik → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your Device.

11.3 Email Correspondence

If you contact us by email, we retain your correspondence for as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry. You may request deletion of email correspondence by contacting us at dotan.simantov.dev@gmail.com.

12. Data Security

We implement the following security measures, verified at the source-code level:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all sensitive locker data, using Android's hardware-backed Keystore
  • HTTPS-only network policy — the App's network security configuration blocks all cleartext (HTTP) traffic
  • WebView hardening — geolocation, DOM storage, database access, and file access are disabled in the WebView; mixed content (HTTP inside HTTPS) is never permitted
  • Notification privacy — notification content is hidden from the lock screen (VISIBILITY_PRIVATE)

Because the App transmits no personal data over the internet, the risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information is eliminated by architecture. No method of local storage or Android system backup is 100% immune to physical device compromise, and we make no such warranty.
