האפליקציה קשניק אינה אוספת, שומרת, או מעבירה שום מידע אישי לשרתים חיצוניים. כל הנתונים (כגון קוד ארון וחדר מועדף) נשמרים על המכשיר שלך בלבד ואינם נגישים לאיש מלבדך. האפליקציה מיועדת לתלמידי תיכון ואנו לא אוספים מידע מבני נוער או מכל משתמש אחר.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Kashnik app ("the App", "we", "us") handles information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Short version: Kashnik does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter stays on your device only.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect IP addresses or network identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting services, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not create user accounts.

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores a small amount of preference data using Android's SharedPreferences system, which keeps data exclusively on your device:

  • Your locker code (entered voluntarily by you)
  • Your preferred classroom number
  • Your preferred cabinet number
  • Dark mode preference
This data is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, never shared with any third party, and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the "Delete All Data" option in Settings.

3. Internet Access and Third-Party Content

The App has internet access permission, which is used solely to load a memorial page from the external website ksharet.org.il within a built-in browser (WebView).

We have no control over that website's content or privacy practices. When you view that page, you are subject to ksharet.org.il's own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing it separately.

We do not log, intercept, or store any data from your browsing of that page.

4. Permissions Declared by the App

The App declares the following Android permissions. Here is an honest explanation of why each exists:

  • INTERNET — Required to load the external memorial WebView page.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — Used to check whether an internet connection is available before attempting to load the WebView.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Declared in the manifest; no notification features are currently active in the App.
  • VIBRATE — Declared in the manifest for potential haptic feedback.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data.

5. Children and Minors

The App is designed for high school students, who may be under 18 years of age. Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user, there is no special risk to minors. We do not knowingly collect data from children of any age.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of this App, please contact us using the details in Section 8. Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end.

6. Data Security

All preference data (locker code, room preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's standard SharedPreferences mechanism. It is protected by your device's own security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve, reset, or delete it remotely. You can delete all locally stored data at any time through Settings → Delete All Data within the App.

7. Compliance with Israeli Law

This App is developed in Israel. We comply with the Protection of Privacy Law, 5741-1981 (חוק הגנת הפרטיות, תשמ"א-1981) and its regulations.

Because no personal data is collected, processed, or stored by the App beyond the user's own device, no database registration is required under the law and no data subject rights requests need to be fulfilled on our end.
